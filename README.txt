Benchmark:

1. Make sure ab (apache-benchmark) is available
2. Copy benchmark to your htdocs 
3. run.bat (Windows only) or run.sh (Unix)

ORM Benchmark (Phalcon and Yii framework):

1. Make sure ab (apache-benchmark) is available
2. Copy benchmark to your htdocs 
3. Configure your database (make sure you have 'benchmark' db on 127.0.0.1, with contents of schema.sql)
3. run-orm.bat (Windows only) or run-orm.sh (Unix)
